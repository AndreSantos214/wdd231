NP Membership

This membership is for nonprofit organizations and has no fee.

Benefits include community networking, basic event access, and nonprofit visibility.

Bronze Membership

Bronze members receive basic chamber benefits.

Benefits include business networking, event invitations, and member directory listing.

Silver Membership

Silver members receive stronger promotional benefits.

Benefits include training opportunities, event discounts, and increased business visibility.

Gold Membership

Gold members receive the highest level of chamber benefits.

Benefits include special events, premium advertising, spotlight positions, and leadership networking.
